# GhostWriter Legal Protection Document

## PURPOSE
This document protects the GhostWriter service operator from liability related to AI-generated content.

## KEY PROTECTIONS

### 1. NO WARRANTY / AS-IS SERVICE
- Service provided "AS IS" without guarantees
- No warranty on content quality, accuracy, or originality
- AI-generated content may contain errors or similarities to existing works

### 2. USER RESPONSIBILITY
- Users own the generated content
- Users are responsible for reviewing/editing before publication
- Users assume all copyright infringement risks

### 3. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
- No liability for indirect or consequential damages
- Total liability capped at amount user paid for credits in past 12 months
- Not responsible for copyright claims from AI-generated content

### 4. INDEMNIFICATION
- Users indemnify GhostWriter from claims arising from their use
- Users indemnify from copyright infringement claims
- Users indemnify from publication/distribution of generated content

### 5. NO REFUNDS (EXCEPT TECHNICAL FAILURE)
- Credits sales are final
- Refunds only for technical failures where content wasn't generated
- Dissatisfaction with quality is not refundable

### 6. ARBITRATION CLAUSE
- Disputes resolved through binding arbitration
- No class action lawsuits
- Governed by Mississippi law

### 7. CONTENT RESTRICTIONS
- Users prohibited from generating illegal content
- Service may terminate accounts violating terms
- No refund of credits upon termination

### 8. AI TRAINING DISCLAIMER
- We do NOT train AI on user-generated stories
- Third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta) have their own terms
